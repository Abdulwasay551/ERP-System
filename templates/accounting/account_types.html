Account Type Guidelines

Balance Sheet Accounts

  • Assets: Resources owned by the company (Cash, Inventory, Equipment)
  • Liabilities: Debts and obligations (Accounts Payable, Loans)
  • Equity: Owner's stake in the business (Capital, Retained Earnings)

Income Statement Accounts

  • Income: Revenue from business operations (Sales, Service Revenue)
  • Expenses: Costs of running the business (Rent, Utilities, Salaries)
  • COGS: Direct costs of producing goods (Materials, Labor)
