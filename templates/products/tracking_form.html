Tracking Guidelines

Status Options:

  • Available: Item is in stock and ready for sale
  • Sold: Item has been sold to a customer
  • Damaged: Item is damaged and cannot be sold
  • Lost: Item is missing or lost

Best Practices:

  • Use unique tracking IDs for each item
  • Update status promptly when items are sold
  • Add notes for damaged or lost items
  • Only available items appear in stock counts
